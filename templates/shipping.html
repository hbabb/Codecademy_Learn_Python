About Our Shipping Methods

Ground Shipping

Standard shipping with flat fee of $20 plus weight-based charges.

  • ≤ 2 lb: $1.50/lb
  • 2-6 lb: $3.00/lb
  • 6-10 lb: $4.00/lb
  • > 10 lb: $4.75/lb

Premium Ground

Faster ground shipping with a flat rate of $125 regardless of weight.

Drone Shipping

Fastest delivery with no flat fee, only weight-based charges.

  • ≤ 2 lb: $4.50/lb
  • 2-6 lb: $9.00/lb
  • 6-10 lb: $12.00/lb
  • > 10 lb: $14.25/lb
